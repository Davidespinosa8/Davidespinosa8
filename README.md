## About me

I’m a frontend developer from Argentina with a strong creative and entrepreneurial background.

My work lives at the intersection of:

- **Frontend development**
- **UI/UX thinking**
- **Digital marketing**
- **Brand communication**
- **Content strategy**
- **AI-assisted creative workflows**

I enjoy building projects where design, functionality and business goals work together.  
For me, a website or app is not just a technical product: it is also a sales tool, a brand experience and a communication channel.

## What I do

### Development

I build responsive and modern web interfaces using:

- React
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS

### Creative & Marketing

I also work on the strategic and creative side of digital brands:

- Landing pages focused on conversion
- Content strategy for Instagram and digital campaigns
- Brand storytelling
- Visual direction
- Social media assets
- AI-powered creative concepts
- Digital product ideas
